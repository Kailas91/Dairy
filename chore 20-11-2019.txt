Takeshi no shiro wa, 1986-nen kara 1990-nen no ma ni hooei sa reta Nihon no geemu bangumidesu.   
Shiro o shoyu shi, pureiya (matawa borantia no guntai) ga 
kare ni totatsu suru tame no kon'nan'na chosen o settei suru kaunto to shite, 
Nihon no haiyu kitano takeshi (Beat takeshi to shite mo shira reru) o tokushu shimashita. 
Kono bangumi wa sekaiju de ninki no karuto terebi ni narimashita. 
TBS no 50 shunenkinen shikiten no tame ni, 2005-nen 4 tsuki 2-nichi ni tokubetsuna raibu `ribaibaru' ga hoso sa remashita.



Takeshi's Castle is a Japanese game show that aired between 1986 and 1990,
 on the Tokyo Broadcasting System.It featured the Japanese actor Takeshi Kitano (also known as Beat Takeshi ) as a Count who owns a castle and sets up difficult challenges for players (or a volunteer army) to get to him.
The show has become a cult television hit around the world. 
A special live "revival" was broadcast on April 2, 2005, for TBS's 50th anniversary celebrations.
